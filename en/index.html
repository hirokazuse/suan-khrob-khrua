Story

From Nong Khai and Chiba:
Two Family Farms, One Vision

We bypass wholesalers and distributors entirely. Every product travels directly from our family farms to your table — and every purchase directly supports the farmers who grew it.

🇹🇭

Nong Khai Family Farm

Nong Khai Province, Thailand — Mekong River Valley

A family farm passed down through generations, nestled along the Mekong River in the Isaan region. We cultivate premium mangoes, Thai herbs, and makrut leaves in naturally rich, fertile soil.

🇯🇵

Chiba Family Farm

Chiba Prefecture, Japan — Holy Basil Cultivation

Our domestic base in Japan, carefully managed to replicate Thai growing conditions. We cultivate holy basil (gapao) and Thai basil using seeds brought directly from Thailand.

🤝

Kagoshima Partner Farm

Kagoshima, Japan — Greenhouse Cultivation

In partnership with farmers of Laotian descent, we operate approximately 15 hectares of greenhouse cultivation. This seasonal partnership ensures stable, year-round supply of authentic Thai produce.
